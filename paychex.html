Modernizing Payroll Infrastructure

Paychex INC • Senior Software and Product Consultant • 2021—Present

The Challenge

The existing import workflows lack the scalability and agility required for modern workforce management. Due to the high-latency nature of our legacy systems, routine data updates for compensation and benefits are prone to bottlenecks. Furthermore, the absence of an integrated validation dashboard necessitates manual intervention to identify and resolve exceptions, negatively impacting our 'Time-to-Value' for new data entries

As the Product Owner, I had to bridge the communication gap between stakeholders, Program Managers and Payroll Specialists requesting updated UI and the engineering team dealing with mounting technical debt.

The Solution

Our strategic objective is to replace the legacy batch-processing model with a Real-Time Data Validation and Staging Layer. This solution focuses on three core pillars: Instant Error Warning , Search Capability and Scalability.
